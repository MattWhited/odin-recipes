Burgers

Description

These supper simple burgers and called that because they are nothing more than some cooked burger patties. I will not be adding anything to them in the way of buns or condiments. but if you feel so inclined you may go ahead and spice them up how ever you see fit.

Ingredients

  • 4 Burger Patties
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • A well seasoned cast iron skillet
  • Cheese*

*optional
