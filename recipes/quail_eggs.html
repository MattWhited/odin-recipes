Quail Eggs

Description

This super simple and super fast recipe is sure to be a crowd pleaser for anyone to takes 30 seconds to make it.

Ingredients

  • 3 Quail Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter
  • A well seasoned cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. Heat up your cast iron skillet. I like to let mine sit for a good 10 minutes before even thinking about adding anything. It is important to a good even heat across the entire skillet
  2. Melt butter in your pan. This adds flavour and prevents the eggs from sticking.
  3. Crack your eggs into the pan, and let cook.
  4. while your eggs are cooking and salt and Pepper to taste.
  5. Once the whites are cooked, flip the eggs and cook on the other side.
  6. Cook eggs to your desired level of done-ness. then enjoy those little cuties. you earned it!
